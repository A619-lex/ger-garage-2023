CLUTCH REPLACEMENTS

Over time a clutch will wear down, and without it, you cannot change gears. The time it takes for clutch issues to appear depends on your driving style and the type of driving you do. If you notice any issues with your clutch slipping and sticking, or your driving experience just doesn’t seem right, then the last thing you should do is ignore the issue, as time will only make the problem worse.
